## Core idea

Make the viewer feel a tiny emotional win in under 8 seconds:

"Day 1: our new dog rated our home 2/10. Then we changed one corner."

The "score" is a playful comfort score overlay, not a medical or behavioral claim.

## Viral logic

- Category: story and identity + product-adjacent demo.
- Context tag: score/scan reveal.
- Audience motive: emotional payoff, pet-parent recognition, curiosity, problem relief.
- Hook: score reveal plus vulnerability.
- Script structure: before -> small caring actions -> score reveal -> comment question.
- Filming method: handheld home footage, pet close-ups, overlay UI, soft b-roll.
- Editing rhythm: 0.8-1.4s cuts, score overlay every 2-3 seconds, loopable end.
- Audio role: emotional framing with small SFX emphasis.
- CTA: "What would your pet rate your home?"

## 18-second structure

| Time | Shot | Overlay | Purpose |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 0.0-1.5 | Dog pauses at doorway, cautious but calm. | "Day 1: he rated our home 2/10" | Immediate emotional hook + curiosity. |
| 1.5-3.5 | Owner lowers blanket and backs away gently. | "No forcing. Just a safe corner." | Trust signal and safety. |
| 3.5-5.5 | Dog sniffs toy/bowl, small tail movement. | "Comfort score: 4/10" | First visible progress. |
| 5.5-8.5 | Close-up of paw stepping onto blanket. | "He chose the corner himself." | Micro-payoff. |
| 8.5-12.5 | Dog lies down, toy nearby, owner hand stays still. | "7/10... wait for it" | Keeps retention to reveal. |
| 12.5-16.5 | Dog asleep, warm light, score UI rises to 9/10. | "9/10: home accepted" | Emotional release. |
| 16.5-18.0 | Same sleeping frame with question. | "What would your pet rate your home?" | Comment bait + loop. |

## Caption

"We thought he needed a perfect setup. Turns out he just needed one quiet corner. What would your pet rate your home?"

## Music direction

- Main track: soft piano or warm acoustic, 70-90 BPM, gentle lift after second 8.
- SFX: small UI beep at score changes; soft chime at 9/10.
- Alternative comedic version: gentle pluck + tiny record scratch at 2/10, then cozy piano lift.
- Avoid: sad rescue melodrama, intense cinematic swells, noisy meme audio that hides the dog behavior.

## Static storyboard prompts

### Frame 1: doorway score hook

Prompt: vertical 9:16 realistic smartphone video still, small mixed-breed rescue dog standing cautiously at apartment doorway, warm afternoon natural light, cozy but modest living room visible, dog body relaxed but unsure, owner off-camera, minimal clean background, TikTok-style overlay reading "Day 1: he rated our home 2/10", playful translucent comfort score UI, authentic UGC home video feel, shallow depth of field, soft beige blanket in distance, no distress, no medical context.

Negative prompt: injured animal, crying animal, cage, shelter bars, medical device, harsh flash, cluttered unsafe room, exaggerated sad expression, product ad, logo, watermark, unreadable text, distorted paws, extra limbs.

### Frame 2: safe corner setup

Prompt: vertical 9:16 realistic smartphone still, owner hands gently placing a soft washable blanket in a quiet corner of a living room, dog watching from a few feet away, low camera angle from dog's height, calm warm light, text overlay "No forcing. Just a safe corner.", cozy home, small toy and water bowl nearby, authentic pet parent routine, soft cinematic but UGC.

Prompt: vertical 9:16 realistic still, dog sniffing a simple toy beside the blanket, slight tail movement, playful overlay "Comfort score: 4/10", small UI beep icon, natural home setting, handheld angle, emotional but light, realistic fur texture, warm neutral colors.

Negative prompt: cartoon dog, fantasy effects, aggressive dog, sad injury, crowded room, low quality text, extra legs, impossible anatomy, commercial packaging.

### Frame 4: choosing the corner

Prompt: vertical 9:16 close-up still, dog's paw stepping onto soft blanket, nose visible at top edge, cozy corner, text overlay "He chose it himself.", gentle focus on paw and blanket texture, warm natural light, authentic smartphone pet video.

Negative prompt: human forcing paw, leash pulling, dirty floor, medical cone, dramatic rescue scene, product label, blurry unreadable overlay, unnatural paws.

### Frame 5: almost home

Prompt: vertical 9:16 realistic still, dog lying halfway on blanket with toy near chin, eyes soft, owner hand resting nearby but not touching, overlay "7/10... wait for it", cozy quiet room, warm evening light, intimate pet-parent mood, no hard sell.

Negative prompt: hugging too tightly, pet discomfort, exaggerated tears, dark sad room, product hero shot, logo, distorted face, text covering dog.

### Frame 6: home accepted

Prompt: vertical 9:16 realistic still, dog sleeping peacefully in cozy corner on blanket, toy and water bowl nearby, warm lamp light, translucent overlay "9/10: home accepted", final question at bottom "What would your pet rate your home?", authentic UGC style, emotional soft piano mood.

Negative prompt: medical recovery scene, overdramatic sadness, unsafe sleeping position, product ad layout, heavy vignette, logo, watermark, unreadable text, fake CGI fur.

## Video generation prompt

Create an 18-second vertical 9:16 realistic UGC-style TikTok video about a newly adopted small mixed-breed dog learning to feel safe at home. The story uses a playful translucent "comfort score" overlay, not a medical diagnosis. Start with the dog cautiously standing at the doorway and text "Day 1: he rated our home 2/10." Show gentle owner actions: placing a soft blanket in a quiet corner, leaving a toy and water bowl, giving the dog space. Show visible micro-progress: sniffing the toy, paw stepping onto the blanket, lying down, then sleeping peacefully. The score rises from 2/10 to 4/10 to 7/10 to 9/10. Warm natural apartment light, handheld smartphone camera, low dog-height angles, soft realistic fur, no distress, no forced handling, no visible brands. End on the sleeping dog with text "What would your pet rate your home?" Cozy emotional pacing, soft piano/acoustic music, small UI beep SFX on score changes, gentle chime at the final 9/10.

Negative prompt: injured or sick pet, medical diagnosis, crying animal, shelter cage, forced pet handling, unsafe environment, brand logos, product ad style, overdramatic rescue scene, unrealistic animal anatomy, cartoon style, horror, aggressive behavior, loud chaotic edits, visible engagement metrics, TikTok UI, creator handle.
