# Static Storyboard Sheet Prompt V1

## Use Boundary

This prompt produced the accepted static storyboard contact sheet for ULT-113. The asset is a process QA / creative demo visual, not validated viral-performance evidence.

## Prompt Used

```text
Use case: illustration-story
Asset type: single 6-panel static storyboard contact sheet for a 15-20 second short-form pet comedy demo.
Primary request: Create one polished 6-panel storyboard contact sheet, arranged as 3 columns by 2 rows with thin clean panel gutters, showing the same dog in every panel: dog_01 / Miso, an adult medium-large gray-and-white Siberian husky, athletic but slightly fluffy, thick medium-long double coat, white muzzle, white cheeks, white chest, white belly, white inner legs and paws, charcoal-gray saddle across the back, charcoal-gray cap over the head, symmetrical gray mask around both eyes, narrow white blaze from forehead to nose, two upright triangular ears gray outside and pale inside, black nose, left eye icy blue and right eye warm amber-brown, plain red fabric collar with a small round silver tag. The mismatched eyes and red collar with silver tag must stay visible and identical whenever Miso faces camera.
Scene/backdrop: the same small clean apartment kitchen corner in all panels, light oak floor, off-white wall, white lower cabinet, stainless steel dog bowl on a small gray mat, one blue chew toy near the bowl, warm evening indoor light, low smartphone camera angle at dog height facing the bowl area.
Style/medium: cute semi-realistic social-media storyboard illustration with believable fur texture and readable pet-comedy expressions; clean ecommerce/demo polish, not photorealistic, not cartoonish.
Panel beats:
1. Empty bowl on gray mat; Miso sits close to the bowl staring into camera with dramatic disappointed expression; blank translucent complaint-ticket overlay nearby.
2. Owner hand only with neutral gray sweatshirt sleeve holds a phone/timer near frame edge; Miso lifts one paw as if stomping beside the bowl.
3. Miso argues/howls toward the white lower cabinet, mouth open, ears forward, same mask and collar.
4. Miso gives side-eye beside the stainless bowl, blue chew toy visible near the mat.
5. Owner hand only begins filling the bowl just out of frame; Miso looks offended but excited, same room geometry.
6. Miso eats from the bowl while glancing back like a satisfied manager; blank translucent complaint-ticket overlay nearby.
Composition/framing: each panel should clearly show the recurring bowl/mat/cabinet/floor setup; Miso remains the same size and breed across all panels; panel borders are clean and consistent.
Text: no legible text except tiny simple panel numbers 1-6 if needed; any complaint-ticket overlays should be blank or simple line marks, not readable words.
Constraints: exactly one dog total, same dog identity in every panel, no full human face, only owner hands/legs if visible, no brand logos, no product packaging, no TikTok UI, no watermark.
Avoid: puppy, different breed, brown/black/golden coat drift, missing mismatched eyes, changed collar color, harness, clothes, bow, hat, extra accessories, extra pets, outdoor yard, vet clinic, cage, messy room, dramatic influencer shot, unreadable distorted text.
```

## Acceptance Criteria

- One dog only.
- Adult gray-and-white husky identity remains visually continuous.
- Symmetrical mask, narrow white blaze, heterochromia, red collar, and small silver tag are visible where pose allows.
- Same apartment kitchen corner, bowl, mat, white cabinet, oak floor, and blue chew toy persist.
- No logos, watermark, official-document style, TikTok UI, or extra pet.

## Negative Prompt Record

```text
puppy, different breed, extra dog, changed coat color, brown dog, golden dog, black dog, missing heterochromia, both eyes same color, missing white blaze, changed mask, changed collar color, harness, clothes, hat, logo, product packaging, TikTok UI, watermark, engagement metrics, official legal document, medical document, cage, vet clinic, outdoor yard, messy room, distorted text, unreadable captions, warped anatomy
```
