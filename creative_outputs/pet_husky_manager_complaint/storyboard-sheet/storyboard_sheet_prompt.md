# Storyboard Sheet Prompt: Pet Husky Manager Complaint

## Use Boundary

This is a static storyboard or contact-sheet prompt for a creative demo. It is not validated viral evidence and should not be treated as final production unless identity consistency passes review.

## Identity Anchors To Repeat In Every Panel

Miso is one persistent dog only: adult gray-and-white Siberian husky, symmetrical gray mask around both eyes, narrow white forehead-to-nose blaze, left eye icy blue, right eye amber-brown, plain red fabric collar, small round silver tag, fluffy curled tail when visible.

Same room in every panel: small clean apartment kitchen/living-room corner, light oak floor, off-white wall, white lower cabinet, stainless steel bowl on small gray mat, one blue chew toy near the bowl, warm evening indoor light, low smartphone angle at dog height.

## Single Contact Sheet Prompt

```text
Create one vertical 9:16 storyboard contact sheet with 8 clean panels for a realistic UGC-style TikTok pet video. The same dog must appear in every panel: Miso, an adult gray-and-white Siberian husky with a symmetrical gray mask around both eyes, a narrow white forehead-to-nose blaze, left eye icy blue, right eye amber-brown, plain red fabric collar, small round silver tag, and fluffy curled tail when visible. Do not change the dog between panels.

Use the same small apartment kitchen/living-room corner in every panel: light oak floor, off-white wall, white lower cabinet, stainless steel dog bowl on a small gray mat, one blue chew toy near the bowl, warm evening indoor light. Camera is a low handheld smartphone angle at dog height.

Panel 1: Miso sits beside an empty stainless steel bowl on the gray mat, serious expression, red collar visible. Overlay text: "Dinner was 3 minutes late."
Panel 2: close-up of Miso lifting one front paw and stomping once on the oak floor, same gray-white mask and mismatched eyes visible if possible. Overlay text: "Complaint filed."
Panel 3: Miso faces camera with mouth open in a husky-talking expression, ears slightly forward, same red collar and silver tag. Overlay text: "Reason: emotional damages."
Panel 4: owner hand in neutral gray sleeve points at a phone or wall clock showing 6:03 near a food scoop, Miso watches from beside the bowl. Overlay text: "It is 6:03."
Panel 5: playful translucent complaint-ticket overlay beside Miso, who sits dramatically in front of the bowl with head tilted. Overlay text: "Requested compensation: 2 extra treats."
Panel 6: owner hand pours food into the stainless steel bowl while Miso waits with exaggerated dignity. Overlay text: "Case settled."
Panel 7: Miso looks back at camera from beside the bowl, unimpressed but calm. Overlay text: "Appeal pending."
Panel 8: Miso lies beside the blue chew toy, still staring at the camera, same red collar and silver tag visible. Overlay text: "Does your pet need a manager too?"

Photorealistic, handheld smartphone look, authentic pet-owner comedy, warm indoor light, no brand logos, no TikTok UI, no watermarks, no engagement numbers, no extra dogs, no breed changes, no collar changes, no changed eye colors, no clothes or harness, no cage, no vet clinic, no outdoor yard.
```

## Review Gate

Reject the storyboard if any panel changes Miso's breed, markings, eye colors, red collar, silver tag, room layout, bowl/mat/toy setup, or number of dogs. Text errors can be fixed in edit; identity drift must be regenerated.
