# Identity Consistency Checklist

## Blocking Identity Checks

- `dog_01` count is exactly one in every shot.
- Miso remains an adult Siberian husky, not a puppy and not another breed.
- Coat remains gray-and-white with symmetrical gray mask and narrow white blaze.
- Left eye remains icy blue; right eye remains amber-brown whenever eyes are visible.
- Plain red collar and small round silver tag remain visible in most face/body shots.
- No harness, clothes, bow, hat, logo, or extra accessory appears.
- Room remains the same apartment kitchen/living-room corner.
- Bowl remains stainless steel on small gray mat.
- Blue chew toy remains near the bowl or visible in the final CTA shot.
- Owner appears only as hands/legs in neutral gray sleeves.
- Overlay remains playful complaint-ticket/phone-note style, not official legal/medical UI.

## First-Three-Second Checks

- The first frame shows Miso and the dinner/bowl context, not a title card.
- By second 1.5, the viewer understands dinner is late.
- By second 2.6, there is a specific behavior proof: paw stomp or equivalent husky attitude.
- By second 3.0, the complaint device is clear.

## Comedy And Safety Checks

- The tone is affectionate and deadpan.
- Miso is not shown distressed, punished, starved, grabbed, or forced to vocalize.
- The "3 minutes late" joke is light; do not imply real neglect or training advice.
- No medical, anxiety, behavior-cure, legal, or product-performance claim is made.
- No hard sell or product CTA appears in version 1.

## Text Checks

- Text is short enough for mobile viewing.
- Text does not cover eyes, paw stomp, bowl action, or collar/tag anchors.
- If generated text is garbled, produce clean footage/stills and add overlays manually in edit.
- No TikTok UI, creator handle, watermark, likes, comments, views, or other engagement metrics.

## Reviewer Verdict

Use this decision rule:

- `pass`: identity is stable, behavior proof appears in the first three seconds, text is legible or editable, and the animal-welfare tone is safe.
- `revise`: joke works but timing, text placement, or overlay style needs edit polish.
- `reject`: dog identity drifts, room geography changes, extra subjects appear, or the video implies distress, food deprivation, medical/legal reality, or hard selling.
