## Core video idea

Miso, one persistent gray-and-white husky with mismatched eyes and a red collar, acts like dinner being 3 minutes late is a formal complaint. The joke is not "cute dog exists"; the joke is a recognizable husky behavior script: dramatic attitude, paw stomp, talking/howling, and owner deadpan.

## 18-second edit

| Time | Shot | Overlay | Purpose |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 0.0-1.3 | Miso sits beside empty bowl. | `Dinner was 3 minutes late.` | Immediate pet-owner recognition. |
| 1.3-3.2 | Paw stomp close-up. | `Complaint filed.` | Behavior proof in first 3 seconds. |
| 3.2-5.8 | Miso talks/howls at camera. | `Reason: emotional damages.` | Breed-personality payoff. |
| 5.8-8.3 | Owner hand shows phone/clock at 6:03. | `It is 6:03.` | Grounds the joke. |
| 8.3-11.6 | Miso sits dramatically by bowl. | `Requested compensation: 2 extra treats.` | Commentable escalation. |
| 11.6-15.4 | Food is poured; Miso eats after a pause. | `Case settled.` | Resolution. |
| 15.4-18.0 | Miso beside blue toy, staring. | `Does your pet need a manager too?` | Comment CTA. |
