Status: local draft while Multica agents run

Use boundary: creative demo / identity-lock process QA, not validated viral evidence.

## Concept

Working title: `My husky filed a complaint because dinner was 3 minutes late`

Core mechanism: breed-personality comedy. The viewer immediately understands that the husky is "arguing" with the owner, and the video gives the dog a mock complaint-ticket overlay.

Evidence basis:

- Round 02 pet case packet suggests `#husky` has clearer behavior-script language than broad cute controls.
- The evidence is scout-level, caption-backed, and not enough for a durable viral claim.
- Identity lock is mandatory because the previous pet demo failed visual continuity.

## Persistent subjects

Use `subject_identity_bible.md` as the controlling source.

Visible persistent anchors for every dog shot:

`dog_01 / Miso`: adult gray-and-white Siberian husky, symmetrical gray mask around both eyes, narrow white forehead-to-nose blaze, left eye icy blue, right eye amber-brown, plain red fabric collar, small round silver tag, fluffy curled tail when visible.

## 18-second edit

| Time | Shot | On-screen text | Purpose |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 0.0-1.3 | Low dog-height shot of Miso sitting beside an empty stainless bowl on gray mat. | `Dinner was 3 minutes late.` | Instant situation and pet-owner recognition. |
| 1.3-3.2 | Close-up: Miso lifts one front paw and stomps once on the oak floor. | `Complaint filed.` | Physical behavior proof before scroll-away. |
| 3.2-5.8 | Miso looks at the camera and does a husky talking/howling mouth shape. | `Reason: emotional damages.` | Breed-personality comedy. |

## Music / SFX

- Music: light deadpan comedy pizzicato or dry marimba, 90-105 BPM, low volume.
- SFX: tiny stamp sound on `Complaint filed`; soft paper rustle for ticket overlay; one tiny ding on `Case settled`.
- Avoid: dramatic rescue music, chaotic meme audio, aggressive barking loops, product-ad style sound beds.
