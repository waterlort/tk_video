## Creative North Star

Working title: `My husky filed a complaint because dinner was 3 minutes late`

Create a dry, affectionate pet-owner comedy clip where Miso, a recurring husky character, acts like he is filing a complaint because dinner arrived at 6:03 instead of exactly on time. The comedy comes from specific husky behavior language: attitude, paw stomp, dramatic talking/howling, and a mock complaint-ticket overlay.

The strongest first-three-second promise is physical behavior proof plus owner recognition:

- Empty bowl plus text at 0.0s establishes the grievance.
- Paw stomp by 1.8s gives the behavior proof.
- Husky-talking close-up by 3.2s reinforces breed-personality comedy.
