## Candidate Patterns

- Role clarity is a powerful shortcut: work, parenting, dating, teacher, customer, couple, and kid contexts reduce setup cost.
- Proof form varies by cluster:
  - comedy uses behavior proof;
  - utility uses result/example proof;
  - product uses use-case proof;
  - transformation uses before/after proof;
  - opinion uses concrete example proof;
  - comment reply uses artifact proof.
- Public shares appear especially strong in relatable, product, reaction, and social-judgment rows.
- Saves/collects are more naturally aligned with utility, product, study, recipe, and repeatable process rows.
- Comments rise when the viewer can supply their own story, judgment, request, or objection.
