## Candidate Patterns

- A faceless account should be framed as a **test bench**, **quality scout**, **repair table**, **home lab**, or **pet-care product desk**, not as a lifestyle celebrity.
- Product proof should precede the product pitch.
- China supply-chain access is an advantage only when shown as quality inspection, teardown, comparison, packaging checks, or durability proof.
- Comment mechanics should be designed by vertical: link/source for home, safety/animal respect for pet, fitment/compatibility for auto.
- Auto and pet have higher claim risk than home gadgets; they need explicit boundaries.

## Counterexamples

- Public TikTok tag routes produced high-metric off-topic rows in all categories, especially home and auto.
- Pet-gadget terms surfaced AI story rows; these are not real pet product evidence.
- Long-form YouTube authority does not automatically fit TikTok if the proof cannot be shown quickly.
- Instagram room/lifestyle prestige does not transfer to a faceless product account unless replaced by concrete proof.

## Recommended Next Test

Create a 12-video faceless batch with no health, safety-critical repair, miracle, or aggressive scarcity claims:

- 4 home utility demos.
- 4 pet product demos or real-pet behavior videos only if ethical owned footage exists.
- 4 auto accessory demos limited to non-critical comfort/organization/cleaning/accessory use.

Measure: 2-second hold, completion, saves, comments asking for source/fitment, shares, profile visits, policy flags, and whether commenters perceive the account as useful or spammy.
