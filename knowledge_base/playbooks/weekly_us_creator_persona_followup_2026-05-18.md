## Updated Operating Stance

The main weekly synthesis still stands: a China cross-border faceless account should be a proof-role account, not a lifestyle-identity account.

Recommended start order:

1. Home/living product test bench: lowest compliance cost, strongest hands-only proof fit.
2. Quality scout / small-space fixer: uses Chinese supply-chain access as inspection proof, not as a sales brag.
3. Pet-care desk: bench-only until owned/licensed animal footage and animal-respect review exist.
4. Auto fitment skeptic: limited to interior, cleaning, organization, and non-critical convenience unless product-policy and script review pass.

Commerce boundary:

- Do not add TikTok Shop product cards or affiliate links to pet supplements, pet medical/health products, auto mechanical/repair/fitment parts, vehicle-safety items, theft-related devices, or any restricted product until seller qualification and product eligibility are checked.
- For automotive and pet products that remain eligible, scripts should still be content-first: visible use, limitation, size/fit boundary, and disclosure before any sales cue.
- First learning batch may mention "testing" and "would I ship this?" but should avoid "best," "guaranteed," "safe for all," "fits all," "vet approved," "factory certified," and similar high-proof claims.

## Script And Asset Rules For The Next Batch

Home:

- Formula: friction/result split-screen -> one hands-only test -> close-up proof -> limitation/source cue.
- Asset rule: table, sink, drawer, fridge, cabinet, or shelf context must be visible in the first 2 seconds.
- Low-compliance boundary: no heatproof, flameproof, child-safety, hygiene-kill, or permanent adhesive claim unless tested and reviewed.

Pet:

- Formula: product/friction -> bench proof -> size/material boundary -> comment asks for pet size/use case.
- Asset rule: fur, water, washable fabric, chew/noise simulation, ruler, and packaging labels can replace animal footage.
- Low-compliance boundary: no disease, anxiety, training-cure, supplement, feeding-safety, or veterinary claim.

Auto:

- Formula: car friction -> accessory in one real interior/context -> hands-only use -> explicit fit/usage limitation.
- Asset rule: vehicle context, product size, fit point, and "not a safety repair" boundary must be visually clear when relevant.
- Low-compliance boundary: avoid brakes, tires as safety advice, engines, steering, suspension, electrical repair, airbags, theft devices, road-use behavior, and universal compatibility claims unless reviewed.
