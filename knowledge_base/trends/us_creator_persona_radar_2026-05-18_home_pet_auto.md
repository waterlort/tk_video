## Candidate Patterns

1. **Proof before personality**: Home, pet, and auto winners all benefit when the first seconds show the object/result/behavior before the creator asks for trust.
2. **Persona can be structural, not facial**: "The organizer," "the lab tester," "the repair table," "the pet-care bench," and "the fitment skeptic" can work without showing a real human face.
3. **Trust is category-specific**: Home trust is aesthetic and practical; pet trust is animal respect and real behavior; auto trust is safety, fitment, and proof of use.
4. **Comment mechanics differ**: Home invites "link/source/where from"; pet invites tagging, emotional responses, breed/behavior recognition; auto invites debate, correction, fitment questions, and warnings.
5. **Product bridge works best after visible need**: The strongest product-adjacent pattern is result/problem/use-case first, then product reveal or source cue.
6. **Cross-platform transfer is conditional**: Instagram authority and YouTube depth can seed TikTok formats, but TikTok requires faster proof and less creator biography.
