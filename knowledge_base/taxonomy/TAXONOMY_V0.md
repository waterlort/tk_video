# TK taxonomy v0

## Account direction

General traffic account first. Optimize for attention, followers, repeatable formats, and audience understanding. Later evolve toward TikTok Shop, affiliate, or product-led conversion.

## Content categories

- Curiosity gap: unexpected result, surprising fact, hidden mechanism, "I did not know this".
- Practical utility: tips, hacks, checklists, how-to, mistakes to avoid.
- Product-adjacent demo: use case, before/after, comparison, problem/solution without hard selling.
- Story and identity: personal experience, transformation, confession, behind the scenes.
- Social proof and proof-based: test, experiment, receipt, numbers, real result.
- Reaction and deconstruction: react to a trend, creator, product, mistake, news, or comment.
- Opinion and debate: myth-busting, hot take, ranking, "stop doing this".
- Entertainment wrapper: skit, meme, satisfying visual, challenge, roleplay, exaggerated scenario.

## Boundary notes

- Primary content category follows the viewer promise, not the surface mechanic alone.
- `scan/score reveal` is a cross-cutting mechanism/context tag and hook type, not a top-level content category.
- `clipping economy` is a context tag for routing and subtype review, not a top-level content category.
- Actual clips, clipping tutorials, payout proof, marketplace/platform opportunities, and recruiting hype should not be merged into one performance family.
- These labels are structural. They do not prove reach, conversion, follower growth, payout credibility, or product demand without mixed-outcome case evidence.
