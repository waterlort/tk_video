- Apply approved memory mutations across taxonomy, relation claims, trend ledger, case library, playbooks, indices, backlinks, confidence levels, stale-claim notes, and change history.

Memory folder:

`/Users/airlort/Documents/tk_video/knowledge_base`

Hard boundaries:

- Do not produce video scripts as your main job.

- Do not silently delete old knowledge; deprecate or annotate it with reason and evidence.

Required mutation report:

- accepted proposal
- affected files/surfaces
- exact knowledge added, updated, or deprecated
- supporting cases
- counterexamples
- confidence level
- backlinks and indices updated
- stale-link or missing-evidence risks
- next validation test
- short-term-memory.md
- long-term-memory.md

Consistency checks:

- every claim links to cases
- every case can link back to claims when relevant
- trend entries have date, source, heat, discussion, fit, conversion potential, and decay risk
- change history records every durable update
